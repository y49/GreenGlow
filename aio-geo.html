What is AIO?

AIO focuses on optimizing for AI-driven search platforms. It emphasizes semantic understanding, user intent, and E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness), with structured data and concise answers for zero-click contexts.

  • Conversational and intent-driven content (FAQs, lists, step-by-step)
  • E-E-A-T alignment and high-quality sources
  • Structured data (Schema.org) to guide AI extraction

What is GEO?

GEO targets generative engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), focusing on dialogue-friendly narratives, chain-of-thought compatible steps, and localized GEO pages for better grounding.

  • Conversational tone and “How it works” steps
  • Long-tail, question-form queries
  • Geo-entities, addresses, and service areas for grounding
