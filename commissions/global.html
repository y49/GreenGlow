FAQs

Do you support localized delivery & on-site installation?
Yes — in key cities and via partners; we also support remote collaboration worldwide.
Lead time and pricing?
Standard: 7–21 days; installations: 15–30 days. Pricing depends on scale/materials/schedule.
What is giclée printing?
We use archival pigment inks and fine art papers such as Hahnemühle (e.g., Fine Art Baryta 325gsm). Prints are produced with color-managed workflows (≥300 DPI), and archival longevity can reach 80–100 years under proper storage conditions.
Licensing & copyright?
Copyright and commercial licensing tailored to usage and term; clear contracts & invoicing.
